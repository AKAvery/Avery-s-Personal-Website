About Me Projects

I’m a freshman at the University of Texas at Austin, studying Computer Science. I started this website as a way to share my projects, keep track of what I’m learning, and create something that feels a little more personal.

When I’m not in class, I’m usually working on coding projects, playing tennis, hitting the gym, or picking up new hobbies like piano, exploring the stock market, or learning different languages. I also just love listening to music—it’s the background to pretty much everything I do.

On the tech side, I’ve gained experience with Python, Java, C++, SQL, HTML/CSS, and React Native, as well as working with MongoDB through Atlas, LangChain chat prompting, AI agents, and user authentication systems. Each project, whether it’s for class, a hackathon, or just for fun, has pushed me to learn something new and keep building.
